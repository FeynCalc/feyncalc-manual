\section{CalcColorFactor}\label{calccolorfactor}\index{CalcColorFactor}}

\texttt{CalcColorFactor[\allowbreak{}exp]} calculates the color factor
of exp. \texttt{CalcColorFactor} is useful for application on FeynArts
produced amplitudes.
