\section{DeltaFunction}\label{deltafunction}}

\texttt{DeltaFunction[\allowbreak{}x]} is the Dirac delta-function
\(\delta (x)\).

Mathematica also provides a built-in function \texttt{DiracDelta} with
comparable properties.
