\section{EpsEvaluate}\label{epsevaluate}}

\texttt{EpsEvaluate[\allowbreak{}expr]} applies total antisymmetry and
linearity (w.r.t. momenta) to all Levi-Civita tensors (\texttt{Eps}) in
expr.
