\section{FCLoopIBPReducableQ}\label{fcloopibpreducableq}\index{FCLoopIBPReducableQ}}

\texttt{FCLoopIBPReducableQ[\allowbreak{}int]} checks if the integral
contains propagators raised to integer powers.
