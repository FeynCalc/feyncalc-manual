\section{FCLoopRemoveNegativePropagatorPowers}\label{fcloopremovenegativepropagatorpowers}\index{FCLoopRemoveNegativePropagatorPowers}}

\texttt{FCLoopRemoveNegativePropagatorPowers[\allowbreak{}exp]} rewrites
propagators raised to integer powers as products.
