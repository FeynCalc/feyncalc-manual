\section{FCPrint}\label{fcprint}\index{FCPrint}}

\texttt{FCPrint[\allowbreak{}level,\ \allowbreak{}x]} outputs
\texttt{Print[\allowbreak{}x]} if the value of \texttt{\$VeryVerbose} is
larger than level.
