\section{FourLaplacian}\label{fourlaplacian}\index{FourLaplacian}}

\texttt{FourLaplacian[\allowbreak{}exp,\ \allowbreak{}p,\ \allowbreak{}q]}
is
\(\frac{\partial}{\partial p_{\mu }} \frac{\partial}{\partial q_{\mu }}\)
applied to \texttt{exp}.
