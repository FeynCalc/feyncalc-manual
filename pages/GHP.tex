\section{GHP}\label{ghp}}

\texttt{GHP[\allowbreak{}p,\ \allowbreak{}a,\ \allowbreak{}b]} gives the
ghost propagator where \texttt{a} and \texttt{b} are the color indices.

\texttt{GHP[\allowbreak{}p]} omits the \(\delta _{ab}\).
