\section{Gamma2}\label{gamma2}}

\texttt{Gamma2[\allowbreak{}x,\ \allowbreak{}y]} is a special product of
\texttt{Gamma} functions expanded up to order \texttt{Epsilon^3} when
positive integer arguments are given.
