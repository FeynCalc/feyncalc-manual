\section{Li4}\label{li4}\index{Li4}}

\texttt{Li4} is an abbreviation for the weight 4 polylogarithm function,
i.e.~\texttt{Li4 = PolyLog[\allowbreak{}4,\ \allowbreak{}\#{}\allowbreak{}]\&{}\allowbreak{}}.
