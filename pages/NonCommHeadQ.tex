\section{NonCommHeadQ}\label{noncommheadq}\index{NonCommHeadQ}}

\texttt{NonCommHeadQ[\allowbreak{}exp]} yields \texttt{True} if the head
of exp is a non-commutative object or \texttt{Dot}.
