\section{NumericQ1}\label{numericq1}}

\texttt{NumericQ1[\allowbreak{}x,\ \allowbreak{}\{\allowbreak{}a,\ \allowbreak{}b,\ \allowbreak{}..\}]}
is like \texttt{NumericQ}, but assumes that
\texttt{\{\allowbreak{}a,\ \allowbreak{}b,\ \allowbreak{}..\}} are
numeric quantities.
