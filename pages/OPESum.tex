\section{OPESum}\label{opesum}\index{OPESum}}

\texttt{OPESum[\allowbreak{}exp,\ \allowbreak{}\{\allowbreak{}i,\ \allowbreak{}0,\ \allowbreak{}m\}]}
denotes a symbolic sum. The syntax is the same as for \texttt{Sum}.
