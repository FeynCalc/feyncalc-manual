\section{PauliIndexNames}\label{pauliindexnames}\index{PauliIndexNames}}

\texttt{PauliIndexNames} is an option for \texttt{FCFAConvert},
\texttt{FCCanonicalizeDummyIndices} and other functions. It renames the
generic dummy Pauli indices to the indices in the supplied list.
