\section{QP}\label{qp}}

\texttt{QP} is an alias for \texttt{QuarkPropagator}.

\texttt{QP[\allowbreak{}p]} is the massless quark propagator.

\texttt{QP[\allowbreak{}\{\allowbreak{}p,\ \allowbreak{}m\}]} gives the
quark propagator with mass \texttt{m}.
