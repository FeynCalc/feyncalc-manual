\section{SUNT}\label{sunt}}

\texttt{SUNT[\allowbreak{}a]} is the \(SU(N)\) \(T^a\) generator in the
fundamental representation. The fundamental indices are implicit.

\hyperlink{sunf}{SUNF}, \hyperlink{sunsimplify}{SUNSimplify}.
