\section{SpinorV}\label{spinorv}\index{SpinorV}}

\texttt{SpinorV[\allowbreak{}p,\ \allowbreak{}m]} denotes a
\(v(p,m)\)-spinor that depends on the \(4\)-dimensional momentum \(p\).
