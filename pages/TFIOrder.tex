\section{TFIOrder}\label{tfiorder}}

\texttt{TFIOrder[\allowbreak{}exp]} orders the arguments of some
\texttt{TFI} functions in exp in a standard way.

\hyperlink{toc}{Overview}, \hyperlink{tarcertofc}{TarcerToFC}.
