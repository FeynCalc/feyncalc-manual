\section{ToDiracGamma67}\label{todiracgamma67}}

\texttt{ToDiracGamma67[\allowbreak{}exp]} substitutes
\(\frac{1}{2} \left(1 + \gamma^5\right)\) and
\(\frac{1}{2}\left(1-\gamma^5\right)\) by the chirality projectors
\(\gamma^6\) and \(\gamma^7\).
