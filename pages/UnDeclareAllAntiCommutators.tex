\section{UnDeclareAllAntiCommutators}\label{undeclareallanticommutators}\index{UnDeclareAllAntiCommutators}}

\texttt{UnDeclareAllAntiCommutators[\allowbreak{}]} undeclares all
user-defined anticommutators.
